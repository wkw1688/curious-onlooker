Favourite Podcasts

Shows I keep coming back to — macro, markets, and good conversation.

Macro & Markets

Odd Lots — Bloomberg

Joe Weisenthal and Tracy Alloway explore the most interesting topics in finance, markets, and economics. Consistently one of the best macro pods out there.

Investing

Invest Like the Best — Patrick O'Shaughnessy

Long-form conversations with investors, founders, and thinkers. Great for understanding different investment frameworks and mental models.

Global Macro

Macro Voices — Erik Townsend

Deep dives into commodities, currencies, and macro themes with top hedge fund managers and strategists.

Markets & Geopolitics

The Market Podcast

Thoughtful Swiss-quality financial journalism with a global perspective. Interviews with some of the sharpest minds in European and global finance.
