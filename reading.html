Recommended Reading

Books, newsletters, and writers that have shaped how I think about markets.

Books

The Most Important Thing — Howard Marks

A masterclass in second-level thinking and risk awareness. I re-read this every year.

Thinking, Fast and Slow — Daniel Kahneman

The definitive guide to the cognitive biases that trip up every investor. Essential reading.

The Alchemy of Finance — George Soros

Reflexivity explained by the man who lived it. Dense but rewarding, especially on macro.

Substacks & Newsletters

Points of Return — John Authers (Bloomberg)

One of the best daily market commentators. Sharp, data-driven, historically informed.

The Overshoot — Matt Klein

Deep economic analysis without the jargon. Excellent on trade, capital flows, and global macro.
